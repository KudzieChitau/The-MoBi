The MOBI is Zimbabwe's very first Mobile
Soup Kitchen, providing nutritious meals and
support to women and children in some of our
most vulnerable and isolated communities.

The organisation was officially launched in January 2022 and registered as the MOBI Trust, a non-profit organisation, in March 2023 .
Trust Registration - (MA318/2023).

The MOBI is an initiative that aims to reduce hunger and poverty on women
and children, with a focus:
Food & nutrition
Health and psychosocial support
Education and literacy
Disaster/emergency response and relief
Awareness, fundraising and support.

The MOBI

The MOBI currently provides in excess of 2,000 nutritious meals per month to children under 15 living in disadvantaged areas in and around Harare. We
that the most effective way of helping others is to understand their plight and gain their trust before forcing solutions onto them. The best way to
achieve this is to spend time within specific communities, developing relationship with them whilst assisting where we can. We strongly believe that, through
the use of the MOBI, we will be able to develop ths relationship and provide long term assistance to the people that we visit.

The MOBI is also equipped to react to crises involving at risk communities and provide disaster relief where needed and is future at all Harare's fairs,
exhibitions and markets where it is used as a platform to raise awareness and fundraise.
